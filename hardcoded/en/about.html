Welcome to the Youth worker’s E-library!

The project “E-library of a specialist in youth work in the CIS” (hereinafter – EL) has been implemented by the basic organization of the CIS member states for working with youth since 2017 with the support of the Ministry of Science and Higher Education of the Russian Federation, the Council for Youth Affairs of the CIS member states, and more than ten partner Russian and foreign organizations.

Today libraries widely provide access to e-sources and organize online user services. The role of electronic materials is increasing. This portal has been launched in collaboration with the Scientific and Technical Library of MIREA – Russian Technological University in order to set up a single publicly accessible database of information materials on youth work.

The primary aim of the project is to provide open access to relevant Russian and international documents in the field of state youth policy.

Our mission is to collect and analyze national and international experience in state youth policy for its further development.

The EL contains normative legal acts, methodological literature, statistical materials, research outcomes, journal issues as well as other sources. Each document is assigned a specific topic. Subtopics are added to simplify the search.

EL sources are divided into two collections: countries and international organizations. A separate block consists of electronic versions of publications stored in the archive of the National Youth Council of Russia. Monthly collections of recent publications on various topics related to youth policy are offered to users.

The search in the EL and the use of materials do not require user authorization. All documents are available free of charge for users on the basis of an open license.

Authors and organizations intending to transfer their works to the EL foundation can send materials to the e-mail turenko@mirea.ru.
