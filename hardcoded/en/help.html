Search instructions

Simple search

This type of search implies information input in the search bar. The search is performed both by title and by author. After forming a search query and clicking the ‘Find’ button, search results will be displayed in the form of a list of sources. When searching by title, documents whose title contains the word specified in the search bar will be found. When searching by author, materials sorted by the ‘Authors’ attribute will be presented.

Advanced search

As opposed to simple search, advanced one involves filtering by several criteria. It appears by clicking on the corresponding button to the left of the search bar. The search is performed by the following attributes:

  • Author;
  • Title;
  • Year;
  • Country;
  • Organization;
  • Language;
  • Rubric;
  • Type of document;
  • Topic;
  • Topic (sublayers).

When searching by author, title and year, a user puts the required query in the entry. When filtering by other criteria, a list with the possibility of selecting a certain parameter appears for each attribute. When sorting by year, time period can be set (from… to…). Under the rubric categories of allocated materials are understood: regulatory and legal; information and analytical; methodical; statistical.

As in the case of simple search, results are output in the form of a list of documents. Filters for advanced search are duplicated to the right of the list of sources.

More about the e-library

Sections

The e-library contains the following sections presented on the main page:

  • Collections. Materials are divided into two collections: countries and international organizations. By clicking ‘All countries’ and ‘All international organizations’ buttons, the user goes to individual pages of collections.
  • News. New teasers are posted with the ability to go to each of them by clicking the ‘Read’ button. This section offers monthly collections of recent publications on various topics related to the issues of youth policy.
  • New arrivals. The latest arrivals to the e-library sorted by download date are presented.
  • Popular sources. The popularity of a document is determined by the number of views and downloads.
  • Summary statistics. Clicking the corresponding button in the site header takes you to a page where statistical indicators such as the total number of e-sources, the number of documents for each of the attributes, the number of views and downloads of each source are displayed.

Bilingual user interface

The e-library portal provides you with the function of switching to English. The English version is available by clicking the ‘Rus/Eng’ button on the main page in the upper right corner.
